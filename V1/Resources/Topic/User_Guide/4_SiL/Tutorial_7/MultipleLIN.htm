About "MultiSlaveCModule_vNet_LIN" Example Project

In this example, 2 C-Code models communicate with each other via LINBus1.

Lin_master has 2 LIN interfaces LIN:0 (Master) and LIN:1 (Slave).

Lin_slave1 has 1 LIN interface LIN:2 (Slave).
These LIN interfaces communicate as depicted in the Fig. 49 and in the Tab. 8.

Executing "MultiSlaveCModule_vNet_LIN"

Follow the same procedure as described in the "Simple_CModule_vNet_LIN" project to execute the "MultiSlaveCModule_vNet_LIN" project.

While mapping, map the controllers under same bus to enable communication between them.

Tab. 10: BaudRate and stepRate combination

The colors specified in the table above specifies the following:

  • Dark Green - Simple Step Rates for 8 bytes payload (each step has at least 62 bits)
  • Light Green - Simple Step rates for 2 and 4 bytes of payload (each step has at least 32 bits)
  • Blue - Perfect Step Rates (each step has approx. 62 bits)
  • Yellow - Not perfect step rates - Master Slave Pairs need more than 2 simulation steps

For more details, refer to the section, "VNET LIN" in the COSYM_V3.4.1_vNET_Guide.pdf document.
